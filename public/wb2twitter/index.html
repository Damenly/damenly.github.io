Wb2twitter

#Sync sina weibo to twitter (multi images supported) #同步微博到twitter (2021)

First of all, things needed:

  • One twitter account with developer approved
  • One linux machine
  • One weibo account (of course)
  1. get your weibo account url. e.g. https://weibo.com/u/2810373291 and get rss address from https://rssfeed.today/weibo/
  2. ssh your linux machine and follow instructions on https://github.com/Damenly/wb2twitter

That’s all. :)
